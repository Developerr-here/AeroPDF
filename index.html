100% Privacy

Your Data Security Isn't Optional. It's Hardcoded.

How we keep your sensitive documents completely isolated and secure

🛡️

Military-Grade Transit Encryption (SSL/TLS)

The Proof: The second you drop a file onto our site, it is encrypted using an end-to-end HTTPS protocol. This ensures that no third party can intercept or view your data while it travels to our secure processing environment.

⏱️

Automated 1-Hour Auto-Delete

The Proof: Our server uses an automated cron-job script. The moment your PDF finishes processing (whether you download it or not), a countdown timer starts. At exactly 60 minutes, the file is hard-deleted from our secure Railway storage volumes forever.

❌

Zero Data Monetization

The Proof: We do not sell data, track cookies for advertising profiles, or use your uploaded documents to train AI models. Our platform is funded purely through simple, non-intrusive on-site ads or premium developer APIs—never your personal data.
